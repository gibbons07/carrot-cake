Carrot cake recipe

Ingredients

















Cream Cheese Frosting





Method

1. Preheat oven to 150 degrees. Line a large with baking paper. Spread the chopped peacans on the sheet and toast for 7-8 minutes. Remove from the oven and allow to cool for 10-15 minutes

2. Turn the oven up to 175 degrees. Grease 2 9-inch cake pans, line with baking paper and grease the baking paper

3. Whisk the brown sugar, granulated sugar, oil, eggs, applesauce, and vanilla extract together in a large bowl until combined and no brown sugar lumps remain.

4.In another large bowl, whisk the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and cloves together. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, using a wooden spoon gently fold the ingredients until just combined.

5. Fold in the carrots and 1 cup of the peacans (the rest are just for garnish.)

6. Pour the batter evenly into the cake pans. Now place into the oven for 30-35 minutes. Test with a knife in the center of the cake if cake is done (if the knife comes out clean its cooked and if it dosen't give it a little longer)

7. Allow the cakes to cool on a wire rack do not apply icing if the cakes are still warm.

8. In a large bowl use a hand held mixer, beat the cream cheese and butter together on medium-high until smooth. Add the confectioners sugar, vanilla extract and a pinch of salt. Beat for 3 minutes until creamy and smoothe. Now apply the icing on your cooled cakes as you like and enjoy :)
